Explore Our FAQs

What is included in the course?

The course includes video lectures, reading materials, quizzes, and hands-on projects to help reinforce your learning.

Who is the target audience for this course?

This course is designed for beginners who want to learn the fundamentals as well as for individuals looking to refresh their skills.

What topics will be covered?

The course covers topics such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, responsive design, and web development best practices.

Will there be any quizzes or exams?

Yes, there will be quizzes at the end of each module to test your understanding, but there are no formal exams.

Is there a certificate upon completion?

Yes, upon successful completion of the course, you will receive a certificate that you can share on your resume or LinkedIn profile.

How do I enroll in the course?

To enroll, simply click on the "Enroll Now" button, create an account, and follow the prompts to complete your registration.
